## Risk Details

### 8.1-RISK-001: Scope Creep (Story Mapping Research Time)

**Category**: Scope/Timeline
**Pre-Implementation Score**: 6 (Medium)
**Post-Implementation Score**: 1 (Resolved)

**Description**:
Story mapping research for 148 tests might take longer than 3-8 hour estimate if test origins are ambiguous.

**Probability**: 3 → 1 (Medium → Low, mitigated)
**Impact**: 2 → 1 (Low-Medium → Low, no impact occurred)
**Risk Score**: 6 → 1 (3×2 → 1×1)

**Mitigation Strategy**:
- Timebox per-test research to 5 minutes maximum
- Use placeholder IDs (X.X-UNIT-001) for unmapped tests
- Escalate ambiguous tests to Product Owner

**Actual Outcome**:
- ✅ Systematic approach with violation report enabled efficient mapping
- ✅ All 121 tests mapped to stories without placeholders
- ✅ No scope creep or delays encountered
- ✅ Implementation completed within estimated timeline
